No monthly fee

There is no monthly fee to pay to qualify for pro level features and support. We only get paid only if you sell something.

No Competion from marketplace.

We do not compete with our sellers. For any product where our customers have more than three choices from third-party sellers, we will pull our listing.

We have no restrictions on selling any brand/product as long as it is part of our Catalog. We may however verify your sources to protect customers.

Multichannel support

Inventory listed on zab.ee can automatically be made available for sale on other channels.

Use your own payment processor

Zab.ee allows qualified sellers to attach their own payment processing (PayPal, Stripe, Braintree) into zab.ee checkout. This way the seller gets paid directly and zab.ee bills the seller with its referral fee.
